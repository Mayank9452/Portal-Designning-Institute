    About the College

    King’s George Medical University (KGMU) is one of the renowned Medical Institutes in Northern India. The university is located in the heart city of the Uttar Pradesh state – Lucknow. The university was established in the year 1911 and recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) & Medical Council of India (MCI). Prof DK Gupta is the current principal of the university.

    Vastu Kala Academy offers many facilities to help its students in growing as holistic individuals, as well as high-class provisions for the architectural course itself.

    • Sports facilities: It has football fields, basketball courts, squash courts, a swimming pool, tennis court, badminton court, playgrounds, and many other facilities to provide their students with facilities to not only grow their sportsmanship but to build skills like teamwork, leadership, etc.
    • Library: The library is the hub of academic student life on campus. King George Medical University houses a spacious library, stocked with more than 35075 books, 2690 book banks and thousands of CDs, journals and reports. The KGMU library consists of a Central Library & separate Department Libraries to help students and faculties alike have easy access to the course and reference materials through a membership card.
