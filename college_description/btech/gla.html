    GLA University Facilities

    GLA Universityhas a spacious and well-planned campus that spreads across a massive area of 1055 acres. The entire campus is neat, beautifully laid out and provides for the academic and residential needs of its students:

    • Hostel : GLA University provides a well-furnished hostel facility to its outstation candidates. The separate facilities for boys and girls, caring wardens and a tight security ensure a pleasant stay allowing you to focus on your academics. The institute has 15 boys’ hostels with a housing capacity of 4300+ boys and 4 girls’ hostels with a seating capacity of 1400 girls. Facilities for indoor games like TT, carom, chess, Television, etc. are also available
    • Laboratory : GLA University has a total of 157 well-equipped labs where the students get hands-on experience on various emerging technologies through which they get an opportunity to expand their practical understanding of how a particular phenomenon works.
    • Conference Hall: .With State-of-the-art auditoriums, GLA University has a capacity to host 3800+ people in 13 auditorium and Seminar Complexes. Students, faculty and guests can easily organize events, workshops and guest lectures. The auditorium and conference halls are equipped with sound systems, projector and green rooms and are air-conditioned to cater to the needs of audiences.
    • Library: The Central Library is the hub of information services in the university. It serves as a creative and innovative partner in supporting the teaching, learning, and research activities. Currently, the Central Library has more than 169899 books over 4,500 CD-ROMs and subscribes to more than 95 National and International Journals/ Magazines.
    • Lecture Theaters: GLA University has 227 classrooms which are built like an amphitheatre, so that the teacher can give equal attention to all the students. 65 of these are tutorial rooms. All these classrooms are Air-Conditioned.

    FAQs

    Ques. Is there any provision for Concession or Scholarships?

    Ans. The University provides merit based scholarships and continuation of the scholarship is as per the terms and conditions of the University i.e. regularity in attendance, progress, good character and conduct.

    Ques. Is there any Management Quota?

    Ans. No, the university only has the following two quotas:

    • General quota: Admissions are based on the ranking obtained by the student in the entrance exam conducted by GLA University.
    • Foreign/ NRI quota: Admissions are based on merit and as per the marks/grades obtained in the 10+2 or equivalent examination.

    Ques. Can an NRI/ Foreign National Students pay the program/ tuition fee in Indian Rupees?

    Ans. Yes, program/ tuition fee can be paid either in US Dollars or in equivalent Indian Rupees.
