    About the College

    Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow (IIML) is recognized as an "Institution of Excellence" by India's Ministry of Human Resource Development. It offers academic programs at postgraduate and executive-level in the field of management. Admissions are done on the basis of various national level competitive examinations like CAT, GMAT, GATE.
    IIM Lucknow enrolls 1,100 students every year across its various courses while the number of applicants number is in thousands. The recent placement drive at the institute witnessed a 100% record in just 3 days. The highest number of offers was made by the Consulting sector (32%) by companies like Amazon, Deloitte, Flipkart, Accenture, etc.

    FAQs

    Ques. Does IIM Lucknnow allow foreign nationals to apply for the program FPM?

    Ans. Yes, the institute enrolls foreign nationals as well subject to the fulfillment of eligibility criteria. However, the students will not be able to apply for financial assistance provided by the Institute. They also need to pay the tuition fee for the same.

    Ques. How is the placement process at IIM Lucknow?

    Ans. IIM Lucknow is one of the premier management institutes in India. Placements at IIML are filled with the participation of reputed companies and organizations. The average salary of 2019 batch went up to INR 16-17 LPA.

    Ques. What are the facilities offered at the Indian Institute of Management Lucknow?

    Ans. IIM Lucknow is an entirely residential institute, offering housing capabilities for 500 students and 75 faculties. It has state-of-the-art IT facilities with global resources that prepare the students with a framework. The library of IIM Lucknow characterizes what the institute stands for – accessibility, vision, and excellence. This spacious 30,000 square foot two-storied library, is a repository of knowledge both historical and current.
