    About the College

    Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), established in 1920, by a Sir Syed Ahmed Khan, is one of the top universities in India with NIRF Ranking of 18th. The university is well known internationally too and ranked by QS Asian ranking at #238th place. The engineering wing of the college called Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology is also ranked at 17th place by India today. The university has opened three new centers of study outside Aligarh in Murshidabad (West Bengal), Malappuram (Kerala) and Kishanganj (Bihar).

    AMU Faculty

    AMU has a large, diverse and highly qualified faculty of 436 members. Each and every member of the faculty is a Ph.D holder in their respective disciplines and. All members are experienced and have gained excellence in their fields. They are always available to students for clarification of doubts and concepts related to the syllabus and outside. The student to faculty ratio at AMU is 15.96.
    The faculty at AMU consists of the Dean, Professors, Assistant Professors and Associate Professors. Various members are recipients of prestigious awards like Padma Shri, Infosys Prize, Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Award, Humboldt Research Award and several others.

    AMU Placements

    The University provides a placement cell that looks after the student’s career. Therefore, students are having a very bright future once they get here. The University stands tall in this regard.     AMU Facilities

    AMU has all the facilities for the overall development of the students. Some of them are listed below:

    • Library: It is known as the Maulana Azad Library. It is the second biggest University Library of Asia. It has a whopping collection of over 11.5 lakh books and documents. There are books on different subjects in Science & Technology. The library has a well-equipped Computer Lab. The library has a great number of books in English.
    • Hostel: An extraordinary feature of the University is its inhabited nature. Both the students and staff reside together on the campus itself. There are 26 halls and 69 Hostels. AMU is really an agent of the country's multiracial, multilingual and multi-religious characteristic. Hindus Muslims, Sikhs, and Christians are studying and staying together.
    • Sports: 19 main clubs comprise the University Games Committee. There are 7 sub-clubs also the country has got the best sportsmen from this University. The University provides for separate grounds for cricket, football, hockey, athletics, and tennis. Games and sports are very important for the students to keep themselves fit and fit as a fiddle. The indoor games are also being played. Indoor games like chess, carom, billiards, badminton and table tennis are there for the boys and girls to play.
    • Computer Centre: It is very well equipped and thus provides with very fast internet system. The computers are updated every now and then. A student can use it for their benefit and thus derive immense help from the Centre.

    FAQs

    Ques. Are all the Programs for Graduation and Post-Graduation available in the online format?

    Ans. Not every program is available online. The University has planned to launch several programs in the ICT Enabled Programs format very soon. This shall depend upon the interest of the students for particular programs. You may want to submit your request if the program you desire does not feature in the available list of programs.

    Ques. Will I earn academic credit if I enroll in any of these programs through AMU?

    Ans. Students enrolling for ICT Enabled Programs earn academic credit, and are graded in the same way as other students of the University. The University maintains parity for students irrespective of the medium of teaching.

    Ques. What are the arrangements of convocation by the AMU?

    Ans. The University facilitates a Convocation Ceremony where students are awarded with their degrees. The students are requested to watch for the notification issued by the University for the Annual Convocation in the Newspapers and in the University’s website and submit the application for the convocation, after remitting the fee prescribed.
