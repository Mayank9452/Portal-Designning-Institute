    About the College

    IIIT Allahabad is a public university established in 1999 in Jhalwa, Allahabad. It offers B.Tech courses for UG aspirants and MBA and M.Tech at the PG level. It also offers Ph.D. in various disciplines, including a plethora of integrated courses. Students are selected based on various national level test scores. With its focus on the holistic development of its students, it provides an environment with all the required facilities. With a placement record of more than 95% placements for the last 5 academic years, IIIT Allahabad shows just how brilliant its teachers are in creating professionals ready for industry challenges.
    With world-class infrastructure, high-level teaching staff, and an attitude toward excellence IIIT Allahabad is an outstanding choice for those looking to be professionals in the fields of Science, Technology, and Business..

    IIIT Allahabad Placements

    International Institute of Information Technology Allahabad (IIITA) has maintained an excellent placement record over the past few years. It recently conducted its 2020 placement drive and achieved a 96% placement record.
    The highest package offered at IIITA was INR. 43 LPA and the average package was INR 20.83 LPA. ITES was the top recruiting sector, and there was ample participation from over 120 companies.
